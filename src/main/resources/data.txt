Shanghai is the most populous city in both China
and Asia as well as the most populous city proper in the world.
It is the second most populous of the four direct-controlled municipalities in mainland China,
with a population of more than 24 million as of 2014. It is a global financial center,
and a transport hub with the world's busiest container port.
Located in the Yangtze River Delta in East China, 
Shanghai sits on the south edge of the mouth of the Yangtze in the middle portion of the Chinese coast.
The municipality borders the provinces of Jiangsu and Zhejiang to the north,
south and west, and is bounded to the east by the East China Sea
